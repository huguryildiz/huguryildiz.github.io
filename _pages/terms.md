## Privacy Policy

The privacy of visitors to this site is important. This page explains what limited
information is collected and how it is used. I will never sell or share your personal
information, and the site has no login, no comment system, and no contact form —
so no personal details are collected directly from you.

### Hosting and log data

This site is served as static files by GitHub Pages. Like any web host, GitHub's
servers may record standard request metadata (IP address, browser type, referring
page, and timestamp) for security and operational purposes. This data is handled
under [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)
and is not something this site controls or retains.

### Analytics

I use privacy-friendly analytics to understand aggregate traffic trends (which pages
are visited, roughly from where) without identifying individual visitors:

* **[GoatCounter](https://www.goatcounter.com/)** — a cookieless, no–personal-data
  analytics service.
* **Google Analytics** (loaded via Google Tag Manager), which does use cookies. You
  can review the [Google Analytics Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  and opt out with Google's [browser add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

You can block or clear cookies through your browser settings at any time; the site
remains fully usable without them.

### Theme preference

Your light/dark theme choice is stored locally in your browser (`localStorage`). It
never leaves your device and is not used for tracking.

### External links

Pages link to third-party sites and host downloadable PDFs (CV, papers). Once you
follow an external link or open a file hosted elsewhere, that destination's own
privacy policy applies.
